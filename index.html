What I Can Do

Need working capital to grow your business? Capital to buy out a partner or purchase an investment property?

As a former banker, I know first-hand the challenge business owners and investors have in obtaining financing. If you’ve been told “We can’t help you” by traditional sources, and you’re concerned about surviving or taking your company or portfolio to the next level, I can help.

Services

FEE-BASED CONSULTANT

With my understanding of finance, economics, and business, I help business owners who wrestle with growing or turning around their company. Every issue is up for discussion, from eliminating obsolete inventory and purchasing vs. leasing equipment to strategies for improving cash flow.

FUNDING SOURCE ADVISOR

I have strong relationships with funding sources throughout the Southeast. My sources include banks, equity investors and life insurance companies. They know that by the time I recommend a client, I’ve done my due diligence on the business owner and I’m proposing a match likely to result in a win-win relationship for both parties.

FINANCING SERVICES

As the owner of Liquid Capital of the South Coast, I offer an array of working capital products. We specialize in Factoring, Purchase Order Financing, Asset-based Lending, Letters of Credit, and more. You’ll deal directly with me, the decision maker. Funds are often available in a few days. No hidden fees or long-term contracts. Our services include all the administration of these services.

COMMERCIAL MORTGAGE BROKER

If you own investment properties (mixed use, retail/office, or hospitality) and are looking to refinance or add to your collection, I arrange funding through Baypoint Financial Services. I’m one of three partners of this business, established in 2013. Our programs include SBA loans, conventional, Fannie Mae, Bridge, HUD, Mezzanine and Equity and more. Typical transactions range from $750,000 to $12 million.
